FAQ

WHERE IS MY ORDER?

We aim to dispatch your order ASAP. You will receive tracking details when your order is dispatched and on it's way to you.

HOW DO I UPDATE MY DELIVERY ADDRESS?

You can edit your delivery details from the 'My Profile' section. Alternativly you can change any details when you get to the checkout page.

WHAT IS ALE-ABUNDANCE?

We are exactly what it says on the tin, we sell and showcase an abundace of beverages from lagers to stouts from all across the world.

CAN I SEE MY ORDER HOSTORY?

Just go to your 'My Profile' page and click the order history button to view all of your orders.

HOW DO I FAOURITE PRODUCTS?

To be able to save a product to your favourites you will first need to register or login to an accout, then just go to the product page and hit the bookmark icon and it will be saved to your list. You can find this list in the 'My Favourites' page.

CAN I WRITE A REVIEW?

Login or register with us and click the 'Write a review' button on the product you would like to review.
